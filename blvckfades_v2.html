    Privacy Policy

    1. Who We Are

    2. What Data We Collect

    When you make a booking, we collect the following personal information:

    • Full name
    • Phone number
    • Email address
    • Appointment preferences (service, barber, date, time)
    • Optional notes you provide

    3. Why We Collect It

    Your data is collected solely for the purpose of processing and managing your appointment booking, sending booking confirmation communications, and providing you with our grooming services.

    4. How We Store It

    Your data is stored securely in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase (supabase.com), which is located within the European Union. Access to this data is restricted to authorised staff only and is protected by Row Level Security policies.

    5. How Long We Keep It

    We retain your booking data for a maximum of 2 years from the date of your appointment, after which it is permanently deleted. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

    6. Your Rights

    Under GDPR and applicable data protection law, you have the right to: access your personal data, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, withdraw consent at any time, and lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.
